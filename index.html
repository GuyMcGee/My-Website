About

Since 2008, I had been in ministerial work, including serving men involved in street prostitution, managing a Patagonian hostel with 10+ nations represented under one roof, and pastoring a Messianic Jewish congregation as a non-Jew. In 2019, my work took me to the Black Hills of South Dakota, where I founded a church. But, being passionate to join the software industry, I changed careers in 2022 and became a remote development intern for Grizzhoot, a gaming company based in Austin, TX.

As a self-taught software developer, I am always seeking new challenges and opportunities to hone my skills. I also have formal training, having graduated from Indiana Wesleyan University/Eleven Fifty Academy’s software development program with a 4.0 GPA and a certification in full-stack development. In 2012, I received a Bachelor of Arts degree in counseling and Biblical studies from Moody Bible Institute in Chicago, IL. In 2022, I received an Associate of Applied Biblical Studies degree from The Grace Institute of Biblical Studies in Duluth, MN.

My wife and I have been together since 2011, and we have four children.

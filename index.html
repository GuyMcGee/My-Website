About

My name is Adam, and I enjoy creating and problem solving for software projects. Mostly, I am self-taught, but I did graduate from Indiana Wesleyan University/Eleven Fifty Academy’s software development program with a 4.0 GPA.

Since 2008, I have been in professional ministerial work, most recently as a pastor of a church I founded in the Black Hills of South Dakota. Though I love the world of ministry, I am passionate to be in the software industry to gain further training and learn from a team.

In 2012, I received a Bachelor of Arts degree in counseling and Biblical studies from Moody Bible Institute in Chicago, IL. In 2022, I received an Associate of Applied Biblical Studies degree from The Grace Institute of Biblical Studies in Duluth, MN.

My wife and I have been together since 2011. We have 4 children.
